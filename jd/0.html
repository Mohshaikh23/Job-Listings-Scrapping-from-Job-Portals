Job Title: Architectural Designer

Job Description

Seeking a talented architectural designer with over 10 years of experience. They will work on sports projects in a dynamic and collaborative studio environment. This position requires a highly motivated, agile individual with strong technical and design capabilities. The candidate must have a solid knowledge of Revit, as there is a significant amount of senior staff available to provide training. The responsibilities include compiling data, performing design computations, and working with senior designers and project teams on conceptual designs, presentations, design development, and production to deliver projects on time and within budget. The candidate will apply creative design concepts, develop project proposals, understand industry-standard construction techniques, building regulations, and engineering principles, support design efforts and client presentations, and develop, modify, or review drawings to incorporate design intent. They will also produce graphic presentations, 3D renderings, and implementation documents, and collaborate on the development of design throughout appropriate project phases. The day-to-day tasks will vary depending on the project's stage, and candidates will have the opportunity to see through the entire project process.

Hard Skills

  • Knowledge of Revit
  • Experience in all phases of design
  • Commercial experience (sports, education, hospitality, large-scale commercial)
  • Ability to compile data and perform design computations
  • Proficiency in producing graphic presentations and 3D renderings

Soft Skills

  • Highly motivated
  • Agile
  • Strong technical and design capabilities
  • Ability to collaborate with project teams
  • Creative design thinking

Job Type

This is a Contract-to-Hire position with a duration of 12 Month(s).

Work Site

This is a hybrid position requiring some days on-site in Richmond, Virginia and some days remote.

Work Environment

3 days in office, 2 days remote
