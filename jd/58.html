Job Title: Business Analyst

Location:Community Place Crownsville, MD. 21032 In-person/ Telework, Hybrid

Type: W-2, C2C

Rate: 45 - 60/ hour

Benefits

Background:The Department of Information Technology (DoIT) is seeking a skilled Business Process Engineer to support our data services organization. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in optimizing business processes, streamlining workflows, and enhancing operational efficiency. This individual will play a key role in analyzing our current processes, identifying areas for improvement, and implementing strategic solutions to drive productivity and performance.

Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Conduct comprehensive assessments of existing business processes to identify inefficiencies, bottlenecks, and areas for improvement
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, analyze workflows, and develop solutions that align with organizational objectives
  • Design and implement streamlined processes, procedures, and workflows to optimize efficiency and productivity
  • Utilize industry best practices and methodologies (e.g., Lean Six Sigma) to drive continuous process improvement initiatives
  • Develop clear documentation, including process maps, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and training materials, to ensure effective knowledge transfer and implementation
  • Work closely with stakeholders to facilitate change management efforts and ensure successful adoption of new processes
  • Monitor and evaluate process performance metrics to track improvements and identify further optimization opportunities
  • Provide guidance, training, and support to project teams throughout the process improvement lifecycle

Education:

  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited college or university with a major in Business, Human Resources Management, or a related field. MBA preferred

Required Experience:

  • At least eight plus (8+) years of IT and business experience in business process engineering
  • At least 5 years in a combination of the following: business process improvement, process analysis and design, data analysis and interpretation, business operations, and process management
  • Specific experience with successful engagements within a cross-functional environment
  • Excellent analytical, verbal, problem-solving, and communication skills
  • Strong leadership and collaboration skills
  • Ability to analyze data and make recommendations for improvement
  • Strong understanding of business operations and processes
  • Experience with business process management software and tools
  • Prior domain industry experience at a state or federal government agency is preferred

Preferred Experience:

  • Knowledge of state government policies and practices is preferred
