ENTRY LEVEL!

Applicants must be able to work on site Monday-Friday in Exton, PA. Hours are 7:30am-4:00pm.

Great opportunity to get into the drafting/design industry! CAD Drafters will get 100% on the job training for the first 1-2 weeks. After the training period, the CAD Drafter will be put on a team lead by a QC Specialist, and will be responsible for doing AutoCAD or MicroStation drafting work!

Job Responsibilities & Requirements:

  • High School diploma or completed GED
  • Experience using CAD to some extent, or interest in learning
  • Desire to work in a drafting role long-term
  • Open to learning new software
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • Associate's Degree or certificate in CAD is a bonus
  • Experience with ESRI ArcGIS is a bonus
