Job Description

Actalent is hiring an Architectural Designer for a large A/E firm in their Virginia Beach office. The successful candidate will detail, organize, and coordinate technical designs to effectively execute architectural projects. The position requires spending 75% of the time in production and 25% in CA/Client facing scenarios.

Job Description

  • Working on 1-2 projects concurrently, with project teams on projects valued between $20-30 million, primarily focusing on base-building for municipal and federal projects, with a current emphasis on local DOD projects
  • Performing basic architectural tasks such as calculations, specifications, and reports, and preparing/reviewing/approving drawings as needed.
  • Independently performing advanced technical assignments of varying complexities within approved schedules and budgets under the supervision of the Supervising Architect.
  • Coordinating technical and administrative activities with employees across disciplines and departments.
  • Assisting in the training and mentoring of architectural and design/drafting personnel.
  • Reviewing and approving conceptual designs
  • Contributing to the development of new architectural methods and techniques.

Skills/Qualifications

  • Revit proficiency
  • Experience in large commercial building design
  • Licensed Architect with 4-6 years of experience
  • Soft Skills
  • Ability to organize and coordinate complex projects
  • Strong communication skills
  • Mentoring and training abilities

Work Environment

The position is based at the VA Beach location office with a hybrid work schedule requiring presence in the office from Tuesday to Thursday.
