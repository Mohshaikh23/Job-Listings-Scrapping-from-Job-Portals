Acute Care Utilization Management Nurse (Remote)

Job Description

We are seeking a highly skilled and motivated Acute Care Utilization Management Nurse to join our team. You will manage facilities for acute care inpatient stays, conduct initial reviews, concurrent stay reviews, and discharges, and collaborate extensively with your team. While the role requires independence, teamwork is crucial for success. Your ability to prioritize tasks effectively will be key.

Your first day will involve a comprehensive 4-week training program focused on learning and development. You will work within TruCare and Interqual systems, with IT and compliance training provided. A preceptor will support you throughout your training to ensure you are on the right path.

Hard Skills

  • Acute Care RN experience (ICU or Medical Surgical)
  • Utilization Management
  • Knowledge of Medicare and Medicaid
  • Software: Interqual or TruCare

Soft Skills

  • Team collaboration
  • Task prioritization
  • Independent work with team support

Job Type

This is a contract position with a duration of 28 Weekly.

Work Site

This is a fully remote position.

Work Environment

100% REMOTE, MONDAY - FRIDAY 8-5
