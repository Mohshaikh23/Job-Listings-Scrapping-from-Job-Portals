Hunt & Hired is a leading executive search firm specializing in finding top-tier talent for our clients. As an Executive Recruiter, you will play a critical role in identifying, attracting, and securing exceptional candidates for executive-level positions across a wide range of industries.

Our ideal candidate is an experienced and highly motivated professional with a proven track record in executive recruitment. You should possess outstanding interpersonal skills, a keen eye for talent, and the ability to build strong relationships with both clients and candidates. We are looking for a hunter who is motivated by money!

Responsibilities

  • Conduct extensive research to identify potential candidates for executive-level positions
  • Utilize various sourcing techniques, such as networking, online platforms, and industry events, to attract top talent
  • Screen and interview candidates to assess their qualifications, experience, and fit for specific roles
  • Build and maintain a strong network of executive-level professionals
  • Collaborate with clients to understand their hiring needs and provide strategic insights on market trends and talent availability
  • Present qualified candidates to clients and facilitate the interview and selection process
  • Negotiate compensation packages and assist in the offer and onboarding process for successful candidates
  • Stay updated on industry best practices, recruitment trends, and advancements in executive search technology

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience in executive recruitment or a similar role
  • Proven track record in identifying and attracting high-caliber executive-level candidates
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong networking and relationship-building abilities
  • Proficiency in using various sourcing tools and platforms
  • Knowledge of employment laws and regulations
  • Ability to handle confidential information in a professional manner
  • Exceptional organizational and time management skills
  • Results-oriented mindset with a proven ability to meet or exceed recruitment targets

  • Health Care Plan (Medical, Dental & Vision)
  • Training & Development
